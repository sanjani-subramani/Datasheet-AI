Randomized Block Design (RBD)
Two way classification
ŌĆó To test the effect of k fertilizing treatments on the 
yield of a crop
ŌĆó Divide the plots in h blocks, each block containing 
k plots
ŌĆó Within each block, the k treatments are given to 
the k plots in a perfectly random manner, such 
that each treatment occurs only once in any 
block.
ŌĆó But the same k treatments are repeated from 
block to block.
